"Blood is that fragile scarlet tree we carry within us."

Who can donate?

You need to be 18-65 years old , weigh 45 kg or more and be fit and healthy

Used for?

Stomach disease,kidney disease,childbirth,operation,blood loss,trauma,cancer,blood diseases,haemophilia,heart disease.

Lasts for?

Red cells can be stored for 42 days.

Types of Donation

  • The human body contains 5 litres of blood, which is made of several useful components i.e Blood,Plasma and Platelet.
  • Each type of component has several medical uses and can be used for different medical treatments.
  • For plasma and platelet donation you must donate whole blood in past two years.
